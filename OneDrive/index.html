About Us

The YouCanHelpHer initiative is taken up by SheCanDoIt foundation. It was founded in the year 2013 by Ms. Smriti to support women who wanted to be financially independent. Ms. Smriti herself is a Computer Science Engineer and has quite an experience in the field. She has helped women who had that desire to do something in life.

Our Vision

The rate of crimes against women is on a rise. On average over 1,000 women face some kind of crime against them EVERY DAY. As a society, we can help decrease this as police and government officials can not be present everywhere but people who form this society are present everywhere. If we see any kind of abuse we can report it so that it comes to knowledge of the police and they can take the proper action required. The sole aim and vision of this initiative is to involve locals/neighbors in making this place a safer place for women.

Success Stories

Snigdha, Bikaner

She reported about her underage friend being forcibly married. We sent a team to confirm and when found true we reported the matter to local police. The local police then looked into the matter and stopped the wedding.

Soham, Noida

He reported that few girls were locked in a room and probably were being prepared for selling to foreign countries. We immediatly contacted police department and got the girls freed from there. The culprits were caught and later the girls were sent to orphanage.

Anonymous, Muzaffarnagar

They reported some shouting or beating noises from their neighbor's house. Our team analysed the isue and did some research around the area. We somehow got to know about the exact situation from the the victim herself. She was wiling to register a complaint against them so we helped her.
